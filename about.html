About Marvel Rivals

Marvel Rival is a thrilling strategy game where players take on iconic roles to battle for supremacy.

The game is 6v6, typically with 2 of each: Tanks, Duelists, and Strategists, but players can choose different roles. The goal is to reach a certain level or score, and as you play, the challenges get tougher. Different game modes are available, and they are chosen randomly.
The game ends when the task is completed, or when time runs out.
